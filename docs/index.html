Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Thank you for using Clean Byte. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Clean Byte handles information when you use the application.

Clean Byte is designed to operate entirely on your device. The application does not require account registration or user login and does not collect, store, transmit, or share your personal information.

Information We Collect

Clean Byte does not collect or store any personal information, including your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, videos, documents, or any other personal data on our servers.

All scanning and processing take place locally on your device.

Permissions Used

The application requests certain Android permissions solely to provide its intended functionality.

  • Photos, Videos, Audio and Files
    Used to scan your device for duplicate or unwanted media and files, display them for your review, and delete only the items you choose.
  • Contacts (READ_CONTACTS)
    Used to identify duplicate contacts stored on your device and allow you to review and delete selected contacts. All contact processing occurs locally on your device.
  • Battery Information
    Used only to display your device's current battery percentage and charging status.
  • Device Temperature
    Used only to display device temperature information when supported by your device.

Local Processing

All application features operate locally on your device. Your files, contacts and other information are never uploaded, copied or transmitted to us or any third party.

Deletion of Data

Clean Byte allows you to delete duplicate or unwanted photos, videos, audio files, documents and contacts from your own device.

No content is deleted automatically. Every deletion action requires your explicit confirmation before it is performed.

Internet Connectivity

Clean Byte is designed to function completely offline. The application does not communicate with any remote server during normal operation.

Advertising

Clean Byte does not display advertisements and does not use advertising SDKs.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

Clean Byte does not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Sentry or any other analytics or crash reporting services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade or share your information with any third parties. Since no personal information is collected, there is no personal information to share.

Children's Privacy

Clean Byte does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any future updates will be published on this page together with the updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
