Privacy Policy

Clean Byte is designed with your privacy in mind.

Your Data

Clean Byte runs entirely on your device. Your photos, videos, files, and contacts are never uploaded to any server.

Local Processing

All processing performed by the application happens locally on your device. No personal information is transmitted to us or to any third-party server.

Deletion of Files

Clean Byte never deletes any photos, videos, files, or contacts without your explicit approval. You remain in complete control of any actions performed by the app.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer through the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
